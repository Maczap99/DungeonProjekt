\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Generator for a random room of a given size and adds doors at given directions.

The generation of the layout can be initialized with a seed to generate the same room again. A room consists of a base\+Floor area and random extension areas to the corners or sides of the base\+Floor. Walls surrounding the areas are generated. Holes are generated at random Floor\+Tiles. Supports are generated in rooms with a big base\+Floor. Finally, doors are generated at the outermost walls for every given door direction.
